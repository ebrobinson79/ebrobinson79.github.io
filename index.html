Caledon State Park

GEOG 777

This application will help you navigate Caledon State Park

A National Natural Landmark known for its old growth forest and summer home to many American bald eagles, Caledon attracts bird watchers of all ages. Trails, including Boyd's Hole Trail, which leads to the Potomac River, are open year-round. A visitor center with bald eagle exhibits, four picnic areas, a picnic shelter and restrooms also are available.

Link: https://ebrobinson79.github.io/GEOG777_Caledon.github.io/

Human Trafficking Portal

GEOG 778

The aim of this project is to provide a easily accessbile web portal for those who feel like they or someone they know are victims of human trafficking in the District of Columbia. Please read through the description and indicators to get a sense if it is right for you. Through the portal, any user can check out the resources around the DC area available to them, filter based on need, route to the closest facility, or submit tips on suspected illicit activity in a discrete manner, should you feel afraid to go to the authorities.

Link: https://arcg.is/0PHrne

  • Date: Summer 2020
  • Category: Web Product
